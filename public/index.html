Add or Update a Miner

Paste the Bitcoin address you use as the CKPool username. If you paste a full worker name, Coin Watch will use the address before the dot.

What “Progress” Means

Solo mining is a lottery, not a savings account. Your best share is the closest ticket your miner has submitted so far; a block happens only when a share beats the current Bitcoin network difficulty.
